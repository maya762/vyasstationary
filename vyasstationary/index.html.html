Our Story

Established in 2007, Vyas Stationary has grown from a small shop in Thamel to a trusted provider of comprehensive stationary and services. Under the leadership of our dedicated owner, we've built a reputation for reliability and quality service in the heart of Kathmandu.

What began as a simple stationary shop has evolved into a multi-service center, adapting to the changing needs of our community while maintaining our commitment to excellent customer service.

Our Location

You can find us in the bustling Thamel area at Galkopakha, where we've been serving customers from all walks of life. Whether you're a student needing school supplies, a professional requiring document services, or a tourist looking for last-minute essentials, our central location makes us easily accessible.
